# Domain Glossary

- Controlled seed data: Manually curated menu and price data used to bootstrap MVP recommendations.
- Budget search: A user query with amount, location, and optional food/fulfillment preferences.
- Confidence score: Internal score from `0` to `100` representing trust in a restaurant match or menu price.
- Price observation: User-submitted feedback confirming, correcting, or rejecting a displayed price.
- Recommendation reason: Short explanation for a result, such as under budget, nearby, matching chicken, or recently verified.
- Seed data: Controlled menu and price records curated for launch coverage.
- Stale data: Menu or restaurant data past its freshness window or contradicted by recent observations.
- Research-only data: Data that may be used for internal feasibility analysis but cannot power production recommendations.

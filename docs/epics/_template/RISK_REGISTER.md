| Risk | Severity | Impact | Mitigation | Status |

## Risk Status Values

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| `open` | Risk is active and needs monitoring. |
| `mitigated` | Mitigation is in place, but the risk remains worth tracking. |
| `accepted` | Risk is understood and accepted by the owner. |
| `resolved` | Risk no longer applies. |
| `transferred` | Risk is tracked elsewhere with an owner. |
